Our Services

We offer a variety of services that cater to the spiritual and social needs of our community. Click on each service to learn more.

Join us for uplifting worship every Sunday morning, where we gather as a community to praise and connect with God.

Our pastoral team offers spiritual counseling and support to help guide you through life's challenges with faith and compassion.

Our youth programs provide a safe and fun environment for young people to learn, grow, and connect with their peers.

We are committed to serving our community through various outreach programs that bring hope and help to those in need.

Our School

Our school provides a nurturing environment for children to grow academically and spiritually. We focus on holistic education and character development, preparing our students for a bright future.
